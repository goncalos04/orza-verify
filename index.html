Your Finances, Under Your Control

Orza is a personal budget manager built around one principle: your financial data belongs to you. Track expenses, set budgets, and stay on top of your money, all encrypted on your device, synced across your devices, and available even when you're offline.

Built for real budgets

Orza keeps things simple: accounts, categories, budgets, and transactions. Just open the app, add your data, and get a clear picture of your finances.

Privacy by design, not by policy

Orza doesn't have access to your data because it was never designed to. Only you can see your financial data, not even Orza can.

Works Offline, Syncs Automatically

Orza is offline-first. Add transactions without a connection and let Orza sync everything silently in the background when you're back online.

See your full picture, instantly

The Orza home screen gives you a real-time overview of your balances, recent transactions, and budget health. Everything you need to know about your money, right when you open the app.

Every expense, perfectly organised

Log transactions in seconds and find anything instantly. Orza keeps your full history sorted by date, filtered by account or category. Always encrypted, always yours.

Set limits. Stay on track.

Create budgets for any category and Orza will track your progress automatically. You'll always know how much you have left to spend before the month is over.
