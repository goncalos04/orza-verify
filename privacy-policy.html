Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2026

1. Introduction

Orza ("we", "our", or "the app") is a personal budget manager developed by Tsukiyo Labs. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use Orza.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address — used for authentication and account recovery. Managed by Supabase.
  • Password — hashed, never stored in plain text.

2.2 Financial Data

All financial data you enter (accounts, budgets, transactions, categories) is encrypted on your device using AES-256-GCM before being stored locally or synced to our servers. We cannot read your financial data at any time.

2.3 Advertising Data

Orza displays ads via Google AdMob on the free tier. AdMob may collect:

  • Device identifiers
  • IP address
  • General location (country/region)
  • Ad interaction data (clicks, impressions)

This data is collected and processed by Google. See Google's Privacy Policy for details. Users who upgrade to Orza Premium will no longer be served ads.

2.4 In-App Purchases

If you purchase Orza Premium, the transaction is processed entirely by Google Play. We do not collect or store any payment information. See Google Play's Terms of Service for details.

3. How We Use Your Data

Data Purpose
Email address Authentication and account recovery
Encrypted financial data Cloud backup and multi-device sync
Ad interaction data Displaying relevant ads (free tier only)

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties beyond what is described in this policy.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Financial data is encrypted on-device before transmission using AES-256-GCM.
  • Your encryption key is derived from your password or biometric credentials and never leaves your device.
  • Encrypted data is stored on Supabase infrastructure hosted in the European Union.
  • We retain your data for as long as your account is active.
  • You may request deletion of your account and all data at any time (see Section 6).

5. Biometric Data

Orza may use your device's biometric authentication (fingerprint, face recognition) to unlock the app. Biometric data is processed entirely by your device's operating system and is never accessed or stored by Orza.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data at any time through the app
  • Delete your account and all associated data via Settings → Delete account, or by contacting us
  • Export your financial data through the app's export feature
  • Opt out of personalised ads via your device's ad settings

For account deletion instructions, visit: orza-verify/delete-account

7. Children's Privacy

Orza is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to request data deletion, please contact us at:
